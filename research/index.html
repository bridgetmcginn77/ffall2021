10-Minute Naan Pizza

Pizza night has never been easier!



All you need is 5 ingredients, 8 minutes to bake


Servings:4 pizzas

Prep Time: 3 mins

Cook Time: 8 mins

Total Time: 10 mins


Course: Main Course

Cuisine: American, Italian, Mediterranean

Freezer Friendly: Yes

Calories: 360 kcal

Author: Bethany Kramer


______________________________________________________________________________________________________


Ingredients

  • 4 naan bread pieces
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded/sliced
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese *optional
  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil, chopped

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 425F.

    2. Add sauce and cheese: Spread pizza sauce evenly over

    naan bread. Then sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese

    and parmesan cheese if you have on hand.

    3. Bake pizzas: Bake on the oven rack for

    8-10 minutes â€“ the crust and cheese should be golden brown.

    4. Slice and serve: Slice and serve with fresh basil, parmesan cheese,

    dried oregano, and red pepper flakes for heat if desired.
