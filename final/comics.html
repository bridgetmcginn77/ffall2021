E.X.O.

Set in a futuristic 2025 Africa,this 40 page comic book follows the journey of Wale Williams, an impetuous young adult who inherits a suit with super powers after his father goes missing. Tricked into returning home to Lagoon City, Nigeria following a five year absence, Wale embarks on a journey to investigate his father's mysterious disappearance. As he gets closer to the answers he seeks, a tragedy occurs and changes his life forever...
