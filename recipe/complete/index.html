10-Minute Naan Pizza

Pizza night has never been easier!

All you need is 5 ingredients, 8 minutes to bake



Servings & Time

Servings: 4 pizzas

Prep Time: 3 mins

Cook Time: 8 mins

Total Time: 10 mins





Ingredients

  • 4 naan bread pieces

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded/sliced

  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese *optional

  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil, chopped





    • Instructions

    STEP ONE : Preheat oven to 425F.

    STEP TWO : Add sauce and cheese: Spread pizza sauce evenly over

    naan bread. Then sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese

    and parmesan cheese if you have on hand.

    STEP THREE : Bake pizzas: Bake on the oven rack for

    8-10 minutes â€“ the crust and cheese should be golden brown.

    STEP FOUR : Slice and serve: Slice and serve with fresh basil, parmesan cheese,

    dried oregano, and red pepper flakes for heat if desired.
